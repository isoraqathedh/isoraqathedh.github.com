Select a station using the drop-down below. A fare table for all destination stations will appear.

Prices are in shillings, pence and centipence. Shillings are indicated with the prime symbol, ′, whereas pence and centipence are written together as a single decimal number separated by the decimal point, ·, and followed by the double prime symbol, ″. There are 12 pence in a shilling and 100 centipence in a penny.

All fares shall only require coins whose value is a multiple of five centipence. If a discount creates such a fare that does not, it will be rounded up to the nearest five centipence.
