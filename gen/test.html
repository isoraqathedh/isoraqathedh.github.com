Feature generator

At a loss as to what new feature your language should have? This is a generator that can help you with your problem! Simply press the button and a brand new idea of what to put in your language will be produced.

This is a language feature which forms a component in feature theory. However, it is not perfect, as it is a simple text replacement engine and does not know anything about dependence or mutex.

Sometimes the generator pops up completely usual ideas, but other times it gives extremely bonkers ones. This is normal and should be expected.
